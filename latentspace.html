I hope this page itself can function as a latent space, where each note or project acts as a latent vector—and through their combinations, both similar and unexpected notions may emerge, revealing something interesting. In a latent space, nearby points share underlying structure; points far apart may still be interpolated, producing meaningful transitions that were never explicitly written. The same should hold here.
Each post is fed through an embedding model to produce a high-dimensional vector, then projected onto two dimensions via UMAP. Posts that share similar content land near each other; edges between nodes mark explicitly related posts. But the space between nodes matters just as much—it is where unwritten posts might live, where two distant topics could meet and produce a thought that neither contains alone. Hover or tap to wander.
