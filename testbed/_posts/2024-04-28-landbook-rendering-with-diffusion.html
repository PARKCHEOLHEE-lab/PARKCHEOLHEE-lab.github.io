Objective

The purpose of this project is to develop a proof of concept by integrating the Landbook architectural engine with Hugging Face's Diffusers for generating architectural renderings. This proof of concept aims to: 1) Evaluate the feasibility of using Diffusers to render architectural outputs generated by the Landbook engine. 2) Experiment with prompt engineering to establish workflows that produce consistent visualizations. 3) Explore the variability of results based on seed adjustments, negative prompts, and prompt refinements. This work establishes the foundation for advancing AI-powered rendering solutions in architectural design.
