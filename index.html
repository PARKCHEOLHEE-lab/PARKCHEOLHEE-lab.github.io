│ View of architecture

These days, it seems that there are so many colorful and pretty buildings. But there are times when I feel like I've seen a building with a similar shape somewhere. Since architecture is a discipline with a fairly deep history, if you list all the buildings from the past to the present, at least one similar building will come out.

However, just because the shape is the same doesn't mean that the design process is the same. So in architectural design, we ask 'why should it be like this?' I think the question called is important. Like all design goals, architecture will aim to overcome the challenges (demands) of the times, such as the physical environment and social constraints. In architecture, there is a site on which a building is located, and the site is exposed to political, economic, social, and environmental factors. Therefore, in architecture, the 'why' comes from the surrounding environment, and design starts from this point
